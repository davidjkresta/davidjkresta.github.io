Prison Architect - Dungeonkeeper-Style

In this version, you cannot control individual units. Instead, lay out zones (bathrooms, kitchens, sleeping areas, outdoor yards) and watch the AI interact. If the inmates become too unhappy, violence erupts. Manage morale, supplies, and security while indirectly shaping their environment.
